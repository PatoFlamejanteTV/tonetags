The tone indicator **{frontmatter.tag}** is used for messages that are jokes.

<br />
<br />

## Why should I use this?

This tone indicator is typically used on messages that could easily come across as serious.

<br />

## Examples within messages:

- buy me nitro {frontmatter.tag}
- let's move in together {frontmatter.tag}
- you're such a meanie {frontmatter.tag}
