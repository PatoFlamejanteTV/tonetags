## Why should I use this?

This tone indicator **{frontmatter.tag}** shows the reader that you're not actually mad at the person you're talking to.

<br />

## Examples within messages:

- ouch {frontmatter.tag}
- why did you do that? {frontmatter.tag}
- it's fine {frontmatter.tag}
- please don't dm me {frontmatter.tag}
- please don't send me a friend request if i don't know you irl {frontmatter.tag}
