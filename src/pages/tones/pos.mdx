## Why should I use this?

This tone indicator **{frontmatter.tag}** represents a positive connotation or remark, such as encouragement or to compliment someone.

<br />

## Examples within messages:

- i'm going to cry {frontmatter.tag}
- would rather not, but you can do it if you want {frontmatter.tag}
